Four Types of graph Classification:

Type A graphs containing cycles

Type B graphs containing a claw

Type C graphs containing an induced copy of a spanning subgraph of 2K2

Type D graphs wich are induced subgraph of P4 or of co-paw

Proposition 2. Let A be a finite set of graphs.

  1. If at least one of the graphs of A is of type D then A-FREE COLRING is polynomially solvable
  2. If all graphs of A are of the same type A or B or C, then A-FREE COlORING is NP-Complete
