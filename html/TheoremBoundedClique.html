Theorem 6.
The following classes have bounded clique width:
  1. (diamond, co-diamond)-free graphs
  2. (claw, co-claw)-free graphs
  3. (paw, co-paw)-free graphs
  4. (diamond, co-paw)-free graphs
  5. (diamond, 2K2)-free graphs
  6. (2K2, paw)-free graphs
  7. (claw, paw)-free graphs
  8. (K4, co-K4)-free graphs
  9. (K4, co-paw)-free graphs
  10. (K4, co-diamond)-free graphs
